Our Story

Founded in 2020 by coffee enthusiasts, Brew & Tea began as a dream to create the perfect coffee experience. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring every cup supports both exceptional taste and ethical practices.

Our master roasters craft each blend with precision, creating unique flavor profiles that celebrate the art and science of coffee making. From the first sip to the last drop, we're committed to delivering pure coffee happiness.
